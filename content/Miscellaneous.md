- Images use convolution, text uses RNNs. Then why bother with MLP?
	- When working with images and text, we use unstructured data. Consequently, these solve the problem of moving from structured to unstructured data (learning using a feature representation).
	- MLP works with structured data.
- Transformers:
	- Encoder-decoder networks.
	- Encoder - BERT, Decoder - GPT.
